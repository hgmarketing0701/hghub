3. Plant, Tools & Materials
MaterialsHoarding panels (MDF / plywood / metal), timber/metal posts, doors & ironmongery, counterweights, skirting, fixings, visual sticker / tarpaulin
Hand & Power ToolsCordless drill / driver, circular saw / jigsaw, hand saw, screwdrivers, hammer, spirit level, measuring tape, utility knife
Access EquipmentA-frame ladder & mobile scaffold / podium (inspected, tagged)
TransportHG lorry for delivery; trolley / pallet jack for moving panels on site
Safety EquipmentBarricades / safety tape, signage, fire extinguisher, first-aid kit, RCD/ELCB on power supply
4. Minimum PPE

Safety helmet · safety shoes · hi-vis vest · gloves (cut-resistant for cutting/handling) · safety glasses & dust mask (cutting/drilling) · ear protection (noisy work) · full body harness (work above 2m).

5. Method Statement — Sequence of Work
StepActivitySafe Work Procedure
1Site induction & permitAttend mall / main-contractor induction. Obtain work permit & approved area. Confirm access routes, power points & emergency exits.
2Daily toolbox briefingSupervisor briefs the day's scope, hazards, PPE & emergency plan. All workers sign attendance.
3Delivery & offloadingOffload panels & posts at agreed point. Team-lift heavy/long items. Stack stable & clear of walkways. No overloading trolley.
4Demarcate work zoneBarricade the lot perimeter with tape / barriers & signage before any work. Keep shared corridors clear for other trades.
5Setting out & markingMeasure & mark hoarding line per drawing. Confirm no clash with services / fire equipment / escape routes.
6Cutting panels / timberCut only in designated area with dust sheeting. Guards on tools, glasses, mask & gloves on. Inspect power tools & cables first.
7Erect posts & frameSet posts plumb & braced. Use ladder / podium for high fixings — no climbing on the hoarding. Tether tools when at height.
8Install & fix panelsTwo persons handle each panel. Fix securely with drill/driver. Keep fingers clear of fixing line.
9Install door & ironmongeryHang door, fit lock/handle, check swing clearance. Door must open to safe side & not block escape.
10Place counterweightTeam-lift / trolley counterweights into position. Keep feet clear. Position so hoarding is stable & cannot topple.
11Skirting & finishingFit skirting & seal base. Use knee protection / good posture. Clear offcuts as you go.
12Visual / sticker installApply sticker / tarpaulin. Use ladder / podium for high areas with a spotter. Tools tethered.
13Inspection & handoverSupervisor checks stability, door, fixings & that escape routes / fire equipment are clear. Record & photograph.
14HousekeepingRemove all waste & offcuts daily. Leave area & corridors clean. Make tools safe & store.
15Dismantling (when applicable)Reverse order. Control panels as removed — do not let them fall. Remove/flatten nails. Bundle & remove material safely.
