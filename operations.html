🧲 Lead Magnet Builder

Turn Google Ads clicks into qualified contractor leads.

The Magnet

  • "The Contractor's Hoarding Cost Calculator" — interactive PDF or web tool
  • Inputs: site perimeter, indoor/outdoor, brand visuals yes/no, duration
  • Output: indicative cost range + 5 cost-saving tips
  • Delivery: email + WhatsApp opt-in

Why It Works

  • Contractor PMs Google "hoarding cost Malaysia" every week — you intercept that search
  • By giving them the cost upfront, you filter price-shoppers vs serious buyers
  • They're now on your list — drop into Follow-Up Engine (Module 10)
⚡ Could be a 2nd HTML page. ~1 day to build a v1.

💼 Offer Builder

Stop quoting 6 vendors worth of stuff individually. Bundle it.

3 Anchor Packages

  • Site Starter — Hoarding + Visual Tarpaulin + Sliding Door. Mobilise in 3 days. Fixed price tiers (S/M/L).
  • Site Runner — Site Starter + Scaffold + Temp Storage. Monthly hire model.
  • Full Fit-Out — End-to-end: hoarding to handover. Phased payment. Project-managed.

Why Bundles Beat À-La-Carte

  • Bigger ticket size per client
  • Fewer quotation revisions (your team loves you)
  • "One quote, one team" — directly answers your Hub's value prop
⚡ Use Claude in chat to write each package — services, deliverables, anchor pricing, guarantee. 1 prompt = 3 polished packages.

📲 Follow-Up Engine

Stop chasing manually. Set the sequence, walk away.

7-Touch Sequence (built in Lesson 4)

  • Day 1: Quote sent confirmation
  • Day 3: Soft nudge — "any item to clarify?"
  • Day 5: Momentum check — slot scarcity
  • Day 7: Decision ask — 3 options (confirm/hold/park)
  • Day 14: Clean close
  • Day 60: Re-engage — "any new project?"
  • Day 120: Final re-engage — share recent case study

🛡️ Objection Handler

The 5 you hear every week, fully scripted.

The Big 5

  • "Too expensive" → reframe: you're not paying for hoarding, you're paying to avoid site delay
  • "We already have a guy" → ask: "what does he handle when 3 jobs overlap?" — your 40 staff = surge capacity
  • "Send quote first, we'll discuss later" → counter: 15-min call before quote = 50% better fit
  • "How do we know you'll deliver?" → black-and-white SOP + photo evidence + named PIC
  • "Can you match [competitor] price?" → not on price, but show what they're NOT including
⚡ Train your team on these. Build them into WATI quick-reply templates.
