🧠 The one rule that runs the whole platform

The colours are not status. They are data layers.
Each stage inherits every colour from upstream, adds its own colour, and passes the bigger bundle to the next stage.
Build every system to read the incoming colours and write only its own — that is the integration.

1 · The 8 data layers

This is the shared language between every system. When two systems "talk", they are passing these colour bundles.

BLUE
born at · CRM (from WATI)
Client / lead — name, tel, contact, communication details.
GREEN
born at · Smart Quotation
Commercial — lot no, lot size, job scope, mall + client details, drawing / measurement.
TURQUOISE
born at · Job Confirmation List
Confirmation data + live job-progress status carried through the pipeline.
YELLOW
born at · Permit System
Approved working permit + worker docs (IC, passport, visa, certs, safety pledge).
RED
born at · Pre-Onsite Mgmt
Resourcing — onsite workers, lorry driver / supervisor, visual, scaffold, fabric, floor protection, client concerns.
BEIGE
born at · Factory Fabrication
Fabrication time, date, worker arrangement + scope → feeds worker pay calc.
PINK
born at · Onsite Mgmt
Evidence photos — dilapidation, in-progress, completed. Direct upload, editable at backend.
GREY
born at · Backend Reporting
CIDB insertion data → manual CIMS submission summary.
